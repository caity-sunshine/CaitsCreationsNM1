About

CaitsCreationsNM is a home based, small business. It started with the idea just to create hand-painted press on nail sets like other young adults have done; however, I wanted it to be unique and different. I have also made greeting cards for a couple years and figured I could do both and sell them. The nail designs range anywhere from special occasions, holidays, birthdays, etc. to rock 'n' roll style, vintage aesthetic, and 70s-80s themed nails.
